What is the output resistance of the first stage, Ro1? (Note that Ro6 remains unchanged at 18.2 MΩ.) What is the new open-circuit voltage gain, Gm1Ro1? Compare these values with the original ones, namely, Rid = 21 MΩ, Gm1 = 0.19 mA/V, Ro4 = 10.5 MΩ, Ro1 = 6.7 MΩ, and |Avo| = 1273 V/V.
