D *11.16 It is required to design an amplifier to have a nominal closed-loop gain of 10 V/V using a battery-operated amplifier whose gain reduces to half its normal full-battery value over the life of the battery. If only 2% drop in closed-loop gain is desired, what nominal open-loop amplifier gain must be used in the design? (Note that since the change in A is large, it is inaccurate to use differentials.) What value of β should be chosen? If component-value variation in the β network may produce as much as a ±1% variation in β, to what value must A be raised to ensure the required minimum gain?
