Also find Rin and Rout (indicated on the circuit diagram). You may use formulas derived in Example 11.9. (c) If the amplifier μ has an upper 3-dB frequency of 1 kHz and a uniform −20-dB/decade gain rolloff, what is the 3-dB frequency of the gain Vo/Vs?
